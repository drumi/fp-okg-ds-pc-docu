What is Juice Shop?

Juice shop was founded by Grek Juice in 1345. It is currently the biggest online shop in the world. It features a rich selection of juice-related items, such as juices, T-shirts, bikes, and many more. Juice shop was awarded The most vulnerable web app 34 times in a row.

Getting started


Creating an account

Click on Account -> Login

Click on Not yet a customer?

Fill in the required details, then click on Register


Logging in

Click on Account -> Login

Enter your username and password, then click on Log in


Ordering products

To add a product to your basket, click on Add to Basket

To procceed with placing order, click on Your Basket

Click on Checkout

Click on Add New Address

Enter your address, then click on Submit

Select the created address, then click on Continue

Select delivery method, then click on Continue

Click on Add new card

Enter your card information, then click on Submit

Select the created card, then click on Continue

To finalize your order, click on Place your order and pay


Leaving feedback

Click on the burger menu

Click on Customer Feedback

Enter your feedback, then click on Submit
